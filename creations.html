About

Master Entity Recording Application, or MERA for short (named after my little sister), is a system of recording major events in the lives of select people and other beings of Olyvia--dubbed Entities. None of the Entities are chosen at random. Each one pertain to the major Fates of our World, for better or for worse. Some directly interact with the Fates while others can indirectly change the Fates. All Entities must be recorded to estimate the possible Fates that are to come, whether that person still exists or not.



Sometimes an Entity strays to far from their course and leads to Fates of death, destruction, and others of those nature. With the help of L., we can rewind the passage of Time and correct any anomalies. These corrections create Time Splits where those outcomes continue in a parallel universe. L. has devised a hub where Time Splits are anchored to our Universe. If Time Splits are not anchored, that Split and Ours will merge and eventually collapse due to the inconsistencies of Nature, Time, and Fates.



Along with the Time Splits, Leylines that cross the spans of Olyvia have created other hubs that connect us to worlds much different from Olyvia. The mystical energy caused by the Leylines become very volatile in such high concentrations, altering space-time and creating passages across dimensions. Hubs are the entry and exit points of these passages.


These passages have allowed travel of beings and objects from our world and theirs. The beings from other worlds that have seeped into Olyvia are known as "Dimensionals". They are sometimes affected by Olyvia's mana, but such affections are minute and have typical allows the traveller to be adjusted to our Rules of Natures. A subset of Dimensionals have been interacted with in their own worlds. These beings I call "Naturals", as they have not been affected by Olyvia's mana.


Inversely, sometimes the mana of Olyvia to seep into these other worlds and create anomalies. These anomalies, classified as "Enigmas", create alterations in these worlds' Time Flows. Enigmas are treated as equals to Time Splits. We must keep a careful watch of Enigmas to prevent potential collapses of other dimensions.



-Bill T. Crepyas
